### What You’ll Need:

* 4× M3×8 mm screws
* 4× M3×6 mm screws
* 4× M3 plastic spacers
* 4× brass heat inserts
* Double-sided tape
* Foam tape
* Electrical tape

Everything :
  ![IMG_6571](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e8dc6caf-a428-4315-b682-bcd3f59c0fc0)
  
---

**Step 1:**
Insert the M3×8 mm screws through the backplate and screw on the plastic spacers.
  ![IMG_6574](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d2514fef-6cb7-464e-825d-927408e1c33e)
(screw them in fully!)

**Step 2:**
Use a soldering iron or the proper installation tool to press in the brass heat inserts.
![IMG_6575](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5af39018-6dae-46f3-9975-74bab90fce05)

**Step 3:**
Finally, mount the display using the M3×6 mm screws.
![IMG_6571](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ff382044-8ab0-4ca7-b9ff-c39a8335c817)
